Signs of Infidelity

Is your partner exhibiting suspicious behavior? Look out for these common signs of infidelity:


- Unexplained absences or lies about their whereabouts
- Increased interest in their appearance or grooming
- Spending excessive time on their phone or computer
- Defensiveness or evasiveness when questioned about their behavior
- Unexplained credit card charges or financial discrepancies
- A sudden interest in new hobbies or activities
- Changes in their daily routine or schedule
- Decreased interest in intimacy or affection
- Finding suspicious items or objects in their possession
